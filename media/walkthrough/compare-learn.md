### Why compare different solutions?

- **Multiple approaches**: There's often more than one way to solve a problem
- **Best practices**: Learn different coding styles and techniques

- **Flexibility**: Adapt solutions to your specific use case

### Learning Workflows:

**For New Concepts:**
1. Read the chapter in your book
2. Find examples in this extension
3. Copy prompts and experiment with Copilot
4. Compare responses to deepen understanding

**For Projects:**
1. Browse relevant examples
2. Copy and modify prompts for your needs
3. Use generated code as a foundation
4. Build upon the examples for your project
